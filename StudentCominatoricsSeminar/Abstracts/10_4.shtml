Witt Rings and Linked Quaternionic Pairings

Kayla Wright

University of Minnesota

Abstract

Murray Marshall conjectured that every finite linked quaternionic pairing can be built from symplectic pairings using direct sums and group extensions iteratively. To investigate this conjecture, one studies linked bilinear pairings and their associated Witt rings. In this talk, we define the Witt ring, linked quaternionic pairings, and discuss Marshall's Elementary Type Conjecture.
