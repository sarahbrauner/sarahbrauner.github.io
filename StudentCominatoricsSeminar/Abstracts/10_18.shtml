Supercharacter Theories of Unipotent Groups

Craig Corsi

University of Minnesota

Abstract

Andre and Yan's construction of supercharacter theory in 1995 has been widely developed to study the representation theory of unipotent subgroups of $GL_n(\F^q)$ where the ordinary character theory is considered 'wild'. We describe supercharacter theories of normal pattern groups and their corresponding set partition combinatorics.
