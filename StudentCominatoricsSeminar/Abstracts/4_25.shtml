Lattice Polytopes and Volume

Noah Speeter

University of Kentucky

Abstract

The Lagarias-Ziegler theorem states that there are a finite number of d-dimensional lattice polytopes (up to unimodular equivalence) with volume less than or equal to any constant K. A new paper by Gabriele Balletti gives a new proof for this theorem which we will outline. We will also discuss the applications of this new proof towards the computational enumeration of such lattice polytopes.
