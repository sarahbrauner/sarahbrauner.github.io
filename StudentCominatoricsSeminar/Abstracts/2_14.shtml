Plabic Networks: Why?

Sunita Chepuri

University of Minnesota

Abstract

In this talk, we motivate and define plabic networks. We begin with the boundary measurement map from planar directed networks to the Grassmannian. Gauge transformations preserve the image under this map, so we transform our edge weighted networks into face weighted networks to remove this redundancy. Path and cycle reversal also preserve the image under this map, so we transform the directed networks into plabic networks.
