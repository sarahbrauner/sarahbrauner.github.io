Cone Valuations, Gram's Relation, and Flag Angles

Galen Dorpalen-Barry

University of Minnesota

Abstract

"Which fraction of the unit sphere in \R^n is occupied by the points z for which orthogonal projection of z into C lies in the interior of a k-dim'l face of C?" This question motivated Carly Klivans and Ed Swartz to write their paper "Projection Volumes of Hyperplane Arrangements." We consider an extension of their result, connecting interior angles of (belt) polytopes to the Whitney numbers of the intersection lattice of a hyperplane arrangement. This talk is based on a paper of the same name by Spencer Backman, Sebastian Manecke, and Raman Sanyal.
