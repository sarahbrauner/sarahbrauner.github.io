Student Combinatorics and Algebra Seminar
Thursday, April 18, 2019
4:40pm in Vincent 570



Multigraded algebra

Andy Berget

Western Washington University

Abstract

I will discuss multigraded polynomial rings and invariants extracted from modules over these. I will explain how Schur polynomials and generalizations of these (such as (double) Grothendieck polynomials) arise naturally in this context.
