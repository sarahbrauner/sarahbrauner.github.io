Zero-Sumfree Sets, Hilbert Series, and Stanley Reisner Rings

Ashleigh Adams

University of Minnesota

Abstract

There is a question in extremal combinatorics regarding the maximum size of a zero-sumfree set. The set of all zero-sumfree sets is a simplicial complex. For every simplicial complex there is an associated algebraic structure known as a Stanley-Reisner ring. In this talk we will both define this ring and look at the Hilbert series of the Stanley-Reisner ring associated with the complex of zero-sum free sets.
