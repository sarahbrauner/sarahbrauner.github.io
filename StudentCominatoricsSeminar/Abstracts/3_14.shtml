Student Combinatorics and Algebra Seminar
Thursday, March 14, 2019
4:40pm in Vincent 570



Geometry of Kronecker quiver Grassmannians from noncommutative cluster variables

Dylan Rupel

Michigan State University

Abstract

The first half of the talk will be devoted to understanding the combinatorics underlying noncommutative generalized cluster recursions. In the second half, we will put this combinatorics to work describing cell decompositions for rigid Kronecker quiver Grassmannians.
