Peck posets and sl_2 actions

Christian Gaetz

MIT

Abstract

A finite poset is Peck if it is rank-symmetric, rank-unimodal, and strongly Sperner (which means that the largest antichains are the sets of elements of a given rank). We'll see that this condition is equivalent to the existence of a representation of the Lie algebra sl_2 which respects the order structure of the poset. We'll then look at some applications: a product theorem, Sperner's theorem, and Stanley's proof of the Erdos-Moser Conjecture.
